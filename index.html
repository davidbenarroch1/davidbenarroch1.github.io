About

I’m a data analyst with several years of experience working with international teams in e-commerce, adtech and brand analytics. My work lives where data, language and product meet: I design analyses, pipelines and LLM-powered workflows that help companies understand their customers and ship better products.

Recently I’ve been focused on LLM-based annotation and evaluation pipelines, multilingual product aspect taxonomies, and insights from large scale customer reviews. I enjoy owning problems end to end: from the raw data and SQL all the way to clear, actionable narratives for stakeholders.
